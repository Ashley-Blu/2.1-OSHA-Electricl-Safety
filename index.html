  • Employer must display safety data sheet.
  • Appropriate engineering controls.
  • personal protective equipment (PPE)

Safe Use of Electrophores Equipment

  • Remove all jewellery before working with electricity.
  • Determine appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Use double-insulated power tools.
  • Use either wood or fiberglass ladders when working with elelctricity.

  • Be cautious of chemical hazards
  • Follow lockout/tagout procedures

Regulatory Info

OSHA Standard 1926.431 incourages immediate shutdown and reporting, in case of electrical incidents.

  • Hazardous Waste Management: Dispose of chemicals and gels as hazardous waste.
  • Non-Hazardous Waste Management: Some gels may be considered non-hazardous can be placed into a closed bag, then into trash.
